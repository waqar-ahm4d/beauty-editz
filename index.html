Our Story

About Beauty Editz

Founded right here in Queens, Beauty Editz was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves a moment of genuine restoration. As a licensed massage therapist with over eight years of experience, I've dedicated my practice to blending evidence-based techniques with the kind of personalized care you simply won't find in a corporate spa setting.

My certifications span Swedish and deep tissue massage, prenatal therapy, and trigger point release — but what truly sets this practice apart is the intention behind every session. From the moment you arrive, everything is crafted around your comfort, your goals, and your healing.

Whether you're seeking relief from chronic tension, recovering from an injury, or simply carving out space for yourself in a busy life, I'm here to meet you exactly where you are.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What should I expect during my first session?

Your first visit begins with a brief intake consultation where we'll discuss your goals, any areas of concern, and your pressure preferences. Sessions take place in a calm, private setting — you'll always be properly draped, and only the area being worked on will be uncovered. Your comfort is the priority throughout.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a 50% session fee. We understand that life happens — please reach out as soon as possible and we'll do our best to work with you.

Where does mobile massage service cover?

We primarily serve Queens and its surrounding neighborhoods, including Jamaica, Astoria, Flushing, Forest Hills, and Bayside. We're also able to travel to Manhattan, Brooklyn, and the Bronx — a small travel fee applies based on distance. Contact us and we'll confirm availability for your area.

How do I book a chair massage event?

Use the "Request an Event" button to send us the details — your event date, location, estimated headcount, and duration. We'll follow up within 24 hours to discuss logistics and provide a custom quote. Event bookings are confirmed with a 25% deposit.

Do you offer prenatal or postnatal massage?

Yes — prenatal massage is available for clients in their second and third trimesters, using specialist positioning and techniques to ensure safety and comfort. Postnatal sessions are also available to support recovery. Please mention your pregnancy when booking so we can prepare accordingly.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Zelle, Cash App, and cash. Payment is collected at the time of service for in-studio sessions, and upon arrival for mobile appointments. Gift certificates are available and make a wonderful present for someone you care about.
